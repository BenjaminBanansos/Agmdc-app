AG Public School

Excellence in Education with Christian Values

The Assemblies of God Public School, located near the Bethel Bible College campus in Punalur, is committed to providing high-quality education to children from all backgrounds. Our mission is to nurture intellectual growth while instilling strong moral and spiritual values.

Our Vision

To empower the next generation with knowledge, character, and faith, preparing them to be responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.
