The Sunday School

Promoting spiritual well-being of children from age 4 to 18.

This department has been established to promote the spiritual well-being of children from the age 4 to 18. In order to nurture a balanced spiritual growth of children, Under the guidance of Malayalam District Council, a curriculum is designed and Sunday School text books are published for each age group.
