Evangelism Department

The main task of this department is to proclaim the Gospel in places where it has not yet reached. Individuals shall be recruited to fulfil this task and teams formed in order to visit houses, conduct open air meetings, and hold conventions.

Members of the churches shall be encouraged to participate and financially support this work.
