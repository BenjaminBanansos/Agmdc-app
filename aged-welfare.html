Aged Ministers' Welfare

This department shall be responsible for the welfare, security and support of the aged ministers of the MDC. It will strive to support those ministers who have physical ailments, do not own a house or any land, are retired, have been neglected, or who are suffering due to financial difficulties.

Aged ministers and their families may be convened for periodic fellowship and prayer.
