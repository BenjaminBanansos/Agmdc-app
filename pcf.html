Ministers' Children's Fellowship (MCF)

This is a fellowship of the children of MDC ministers. There are ministers’ children who have not received needed recognition and care during their parents’ ministry. Still there are others who could not progress in their lives due to the financial constraints of their parents’ ministry. The AG MCF has been created to provide fellowship, mutual support, and care for ministers’ children.
