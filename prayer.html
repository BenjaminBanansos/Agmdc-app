Prayer Department

Revive us again, O God! I know you will! Give us a fresh start! (Psalms 85:6). That is the purpose of this Department, a revival in India, Under the guidance and leadership of Rev. T J Samuel (Superintendent AGMDC).

The goal of this department is to equip, enable, and encourage churches / individuals to pray continually and effectively while making prayer a part of everything they do. Prayer is a powerful tool to demonstrate God’s power in the world.
