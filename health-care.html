Health Care Department

This team is comprised of trained and experienced medical doctors, nurses, and other medical professionals. The department emphasizes the service of the church in the field of health care.

Medical camps may be organized where needed. It may also conduct various types of awareness classes for local communities. Short term clinics may be organized in rural areas. This team may also serve whenever there are public programs organized by the MDC.
