Women's Missionary Council (WMC)

The Women's Missionary Council is the women's wing of the Assemblies of God. We are dedicated to the spiritual growth, empowerment, and active participation of women in the mission of the church.

Our Mission

"To Reach, Teach, and Keep Women for the Glory of God."

Key Activities

  • Prayer Networks

    Organizing chain prayers, fasting prayers, and intercessory groups across local churches to uphold the ministry and families.

  • Charitable Initiatives

    Providing financial aid for the education of poor children, marriage of girls from poor families, and constructing houses for the homeless.

  • Annual Conferences

    Gatherings focused on spiritual renewal, Bible study, and leadership training for women.
