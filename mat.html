Ministers' Advanced Training Institute

This is programme directly designed and run by the Executive Committee of the Malayalam district Council for all ministers of the MDC. Its main objectives are: deepening their commitment in personal piety, updating them on the modern developments in various fields, to sharpen up the skills of serving the church, and train them “to equip the saints for the service of their ministry.”

The High Range Trinity Bible College, Anakkara near Kumily in Idukki district is intended as venue of this five days camp.
