Bethel Bible College

Bethel Bible College is the major Theological school of the Malayalam District, located on a beautiful campus of 7 acre on a hill near the Railway Station in Punalur in the Kollam District of Kerala, India.

Founded in 1927, it is the first Bible School of the Assemblies of God outside the USA. It offers various accredited programs including B.Th., M.Div., M.Th., and B.D. (affiliated with the Senate of Serampore University).

Bethel has been instrumental in providing leaders for the AG and its graduates serve globally.
