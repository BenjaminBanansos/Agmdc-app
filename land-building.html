Land And Building Monitoring

This department shall evaluate the suitability and legal validity of the land to be bought for local congregations of the Malayalam District Council and make recommendations to the appropriate authorities.

This Department is responsible for ensuring that proper documentation for properties is maintained, and for submitting said records to the AG MDC Office. When buildings are constructed for a local church, this department shall evaluate the plans and submit their recommendations to the Executive Committee.

This department shall make sure that property taxes are paid annually by the local churches.
