Christ's Ambassadors (CA)

Encouraging holisitic development of youths.

The Christian Association (CA) has been established to encourage the holistic development of youths, enabling them to effectively witness the gospel of Jesus Christ among their peers. It also promotes the development of God-given talents in young people, so they can contribute to the establishment of God's Kingdom.

The District CA Committee, comprising a President, Secretary, Treasurer, and other committee members, will systematically organize the activities of this department.
