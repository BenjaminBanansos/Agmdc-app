Church Tribunal

If there is an appeal against the decision of the Section committee or regional director on a complaint submitted by a local church member, he/she may appeal to the Executive Committee for further action.

If the Executive Committee is convinced of the unresolved grievance, it may refer the matter to the Tribunal; and the Tribunal, after hearing the case, shall make recommendations to the Executive Committee for further action. The decision of the Executive Committee shall be final.
